# LinkedIn Content

## Overview

LinkedIn is Seeker's highest-intent channel. The audience skews toward employed professionals and recent graduates quietly evaluating their market position, which is exactly the "upload once, see where you rank" moment Seeker is built for. Posts are scheduled through **Buffer** on a Mon/Wed/Fri, 9am PT cadence, timed to when this audience is actively scrolling between meetings rather than deep in focus work.

The three posts here aren't random content, they form a small arc: introduce the mechanism (fit over keywords), give a reason to act right now (a rank-based hook), then hand off to owned content for anyone not ready to upload a resume yet.

## Notes

All three posts follow Seeker's core content rule: lead with data the platform itself produces, not advice a competitor could publish from public job board listings. The Guides post exists specifically to give LinkedIn a lower-commitment next step, since not everyone who sees a post is ready to hand over a resume on the spot.
