# Outreach Research

## Overview

I supported outreach research for Seeker: identifying individuals, communities, and platforms whose audience could genuinely benefit from the product, and organizing candidates for review before any outreach was sent. Outreach was done in small, reviewed batches rather than at bulk scale, with an emphasis on relevance and personalization over volume.
