# Outreach Research

## Overview

I supported outreach research and partnership discovery for Seeker: identifying individuals, communities, and platforms whose audience could genuinely benefit from the product, then organizing candidates for review before any outreach was sent. The emphasis throughout was relevance and personalization over volume, a small number of well-researched contacts over a large, low-quality list.

## Approach

Candidates were sourced and reviewed in small batches ("waves"), each checked for audience fit before being added to an outreach queue. Every wave was tracked so progress could be reviewed over time rather than treated as a one-off push.
